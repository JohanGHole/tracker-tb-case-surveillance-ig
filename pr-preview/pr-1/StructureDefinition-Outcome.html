Logical Model: Outcome

Official URL: http://example.org/StructureDefinition/Outcome Version: 0.1.0
Draft as of 2024-12-18 Computable Name: Outcome

Represents the final outcome of the TB case, including case closure, treatment success, or necessary corrections.
