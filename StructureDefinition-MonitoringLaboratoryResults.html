Logical Model: Monitoring laboratory results

Official URL: http://example.org/StructureDefinition/MonitoringLaboratoryResults Version: 0.1.0
Draft as of 2025-01-06 Computable Name: MonitoringLaboratoryResults

Captures laboratory test results for monitoring treatment progress, including sputum microscopy and culture tests for drug-resistant TB cases.

Usage:

  • Use this Logical Model Profile: TB Case Surveillance
