Official URL: http://example.org/StructureDefinition/TBTreatment Version: 0.1.0
Draft as of 2024-12-18 Computable Name: TBTreatment

Tracks the patient’s treatment regimen, including details of medication, dosages, and any updates to the treatment plan.
