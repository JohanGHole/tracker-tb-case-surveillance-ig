This page provides a list of the FHIR artifacts defined as part of this implementation guide.

Structures: Logical Models

These define data models that represent the domain covered by this implementation guide in more business-friendly terms than the underlying FHIR resources.

Diagnosis and notification

Represents the collection of baseline patient data, risk factors, and the diagnostic decision, including notifications of confirmed or ruled-out cases.
Diagnostic laboratory results

Captures diagnostic test results along with sample information to determine TB infection status.
Monitoring laboratory results

Captures laboratory test results for monitoring treatment progress, including sputum microscopy and culture tests for drug-resistant TB cases.
Outcome

Represents the final outcome of the TB case, including case closure, treatment success, or necessary corrections.
TB Case Surveillance

Represents the overall structure of the program and the initial capture of patient demographcis and key identifiers.
TB Treatment

Tracks the patient's treatment regimen, including details of medication, dosages, and any updates to the treatment plan.

Structures: Questionnaires

These define forms used by systems conforming to this implementation guide to capture or expose data to end users.

Diagnosis and Notification Questionnaire

Represents the collection of baseline patient data, risk factors, and the diagnostic decision, including notifications of confirmed or ruled-out cases.
Diagnostic Laboratory Results Questionnaire

A questionnaire for capturing diagnostic test results and sample information to determine TB infection status.
Monitoring Laboratory Results Questionnaire

A questionnaire for capturing laboratory test results to monitor treatment progress, including sputum microscopy and culture tests for drug-resistant TB cases.
TB Case Surveillance Questionnaire

A questionnaire for TB Case Surveillance capturing patient demographics and program-specific information.
TB Outcome Questionnaire

A questionnaire for capturing the final outcome of the TB case, including case closure, treatment success, or necessary corrections.
TB Treatment Questionnaire

A questionnaire for capturing details of the patient’s treatment regimen, including medications, dosages, and updates to the treatment plan.
