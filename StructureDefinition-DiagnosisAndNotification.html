Logical Model: Diagnosis and notification

Official URL: http://example.org/StructureDefinition/DiagnosisAndNotification Version: 0.1.0
Draft as of 2024-12-19 Computable Name: DiagnosisAndNotification

Represents the collection of baseline patient data, risk factors, and the diagnostic decision, including notifications of confirmed or ruled-out cases.

Usage:

  • Use this Logical Model Profile: TB Case Surveillance
